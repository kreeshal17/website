Childhood

During my childhood, I was a child full of curiosity, spending my time learning about new things and technology. I was somewhat introverted, often finding solace in books, immersing myself in their pages to explore new worlds and ideas.

School

My 12-year journey at Sunshine School has been enriching and transformative. With its emphasis on academic excellence and nurturing environment, Sunshine has provided me with a strong foundation for learning and personal growth. I'm grateful for the supportive community of teachers and peers who have contributed to my success, and I cherish the invaluable memories and lessons learned during my time there.

College

During my college years at CCRC, one of Nepal's prestigious institutions renowned for its excellence in education and outstanding alumni network, I was provided with unparalleled opportunities to enhance my knowledge and skills. CCRC's reputation for producing top-notch graduates, coupled with its impressive ranking by the Institute of Engineering (IOE), solidified my decision to pursue my education there. Immersed in a dynamic learning environment, I seized every opportunity to engage with challenging coursework, participate in enriching extracurricular activities, and collaborate with driven peers. My time at CCRC not only broadened my academic horizons but also fostered personal growth, preparing me for the diverse challenges of the future.

University

As a Jain student at Jain University, my academic journey has been enriched in numerous ways. The university's commitment to holistic education has provided me with a supportive environment where I've been able to thrive both academically and personally. Through its diverse range of programs and extracurricular activities, I've had the opportunity to explore my interests and passions, gaining valuable knowledge and skills along the way. One of the most significant ways Jain University has helped me is by fostering a sense of community and belonging. As a member of the Jain student body, I've found camaraderie among peers who share similar values and beliefs. This sense of unity has not only enhanced my academic experience but has also provided me with a strong support system that has been invaluable throughout my journey..
